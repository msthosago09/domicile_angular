About Us

Putting people and planet first

Concern for the safety of people and the sustainability of our environment. That is what drives Domicile as a full-service, integrated consulting firm specialising in occupational health, safety and environment (OHSE) in the construction, mining and manufacturing sectors. We are a 100% black female-owned company that comprises a committed team of young professionals. We pride ourselves on our astute work ethic, on the exceptional quality of our work and on our sound client relationships. Our client-centric approach involves more than simply respecting and valuing our clients’ time and resources; we aim to truly understand their aspirations in undertaking the projects they do, so we may share those goals in bringing their vision to life. These qualities have nurtured our solid reputation in the industry for consistently providing cost-efficient OHSE solutions, as well as delivering completed projects that are on time, within scope and on budget.

Specialised skills

All the employees within our team are provided with basic training to ensure a solid foundation from which to grow their areas of specialty and perform their key responsibilities. Over and above these employees have extensive experience in their respective fields within the construction, mining and manufacturing industries. Our OHS agents are registered with the South African Council for the Projects and Construction Management Professions (SACPCMP), and our safety officer and representatives are registered with South African Institute of Occupational Safety and Health (SAIOSH). Our full-service team further comprises qualified environmentalists, risk managers, statisticians with a minimum three years’ industry experience.

Regulatory compliance

Every project we undertake is done so within the ambit of current legislation. Our teams are familiar with all relevant regulatory requirements, can advise clients on issues of compliance, and will ensure every project meets required legislative obligations. This includes compliance with: Relevant occupational health and safety legislation: Occupational Health and Safety Act and Regulations 85 of 1993 Compensation for Occupational Injuries and Diseases Act Basic Conditions of Employment Act Labour Relations Act Employment Equity Act Relevant environmental legislation: The National Environmental Management Act 107 0f 1998 National Environmental Management: Air Quality Act National Environmental Management: Waste Act National Water Act
